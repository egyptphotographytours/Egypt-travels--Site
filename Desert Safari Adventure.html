Sharm El Sheikh: Desert Safari

Detailed Itinerary

  • **Afternoon:** Pick up from your Sharm El Sheikh hotel. Drive into the heart of the Sinai desert.
  • **Late Afternoon:** Enjoy a thrilling **quad bike ride** across the sand dunes. Experience the vast, serene landscape of the desert.
  • **Sunset:** Go on a relaxing **camel ride** to a traditional Bedouin camp and watch the beautiful desert sunset.
  • **Evening:** Enjoy a delicious **Bedouin BBQ dinner** under the stars, accompanied by a traditional show with music and fire dancing.
  • **Night:** Transfer back to your hotel.

Inclusions

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off.
  • Quad bike and camel ride.
  • Traditional Bedouin dinner and soft drinks.
  • Live show at the camp.
  • All taxes and fees.

Exclusions

  • Gratuities (optional).
  • Personal expenses.
