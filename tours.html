Frequently Asked Questions about Egypt Tours

What are the best tours in Egypt for a first-time visitor?

For a first-time trip to Egypt, we highly recommend our **Giza, Saqqara & Memphis** tour to see the iconic pyramids and our **Cairo: Museum, Citadel & Bazaar** tour for a comprehensive view of the capital. If you have more time, combining these with a **Luxor West Bank** tour is the perfect way to experience the highlights of ancient Egypt.

Are private tours in Egypt safe?

Yes, our private tours are exceptionally safe. We work with highly-vetted, professional drivers and certified guides. Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. We follow all local regulations and ensure a seamless, worry-free experience for all our guests. You are always in good hands with our team.

What is the best time to travel to Egypt?

The best time to visit Egypt is during the cooler months, from **October to April**. The weather is pleasant for sightseeing, especially in places like Luxor and Aswan where it can get extremely hot in the summer. However, the summer months (May to September) are also a great time to visit coastal areas like the **Red Sea** for diving and beach activities.
