Hurghada: Snorkeling & Island Trip

Detailed Itinerary

  • **Morning:** Pick up from your Hurghada hotel. Board a boat and set sail into the pristine waters of the Red Sea.
  • **Midday:** Stop at two different snorkeling spots to discover colorful coral reefs and exotic fish. Snorkeling gear is provided. Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch on board the boat.
  • **Afternoon:** Relax on a secluded island or swim in its shallow turquoise waters. Take photos and soak up the sun.
  • **Evening:** Return to the marina and transfer back to your hotel.

Inclusions

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off.
  • Boat trip with two snorkeling stops.
  • Snorkeling equipment.
  • Lunch and soft drinks on board.
  • All taxes and fees.

Exclusions

  • Gratuities (optional).
  • Personal expenses.
